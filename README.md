## About Me

Senior **Django / FastAPI** backend developer with **9+ years** of experience building clean, maintainable systems for production web applications. I specialize in **REST APIs**, **microservice architectures**, **PostgreSQL / Redis**, and **observability** (Elasticsearch, Prometheus, Grafana) — with a strong focus on **OOP**, **SOLID**, and **TDD**.

Currently at **MCI Next**, working on a microservice platform serving **~500K daily active users** — including Authentication, AI Assistant, Wallet & Transactions, Router, and Uploader services. Previously led FastAPI backend development across large-scale distributed systems at **Amin Etemad**.

I enjoy contributing to open source and building practical side projects that combine automation, data pipelines, and solid backend engineering.
